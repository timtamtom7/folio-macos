# Folio — Onboarding

4 screens for the RSS reader.

---

## Screen 1 — Welcome to Folio

**Headline:** Your reading, distraction-free.

**Body:** Folio brings all your favorite blogs, newsletters, and feeds into one calm, beautiful space. No algorithms, no ads — just the content you choose.

**Illustration:** A flat, modern illustration of an open magazine/book on a soft warm background. Subtle paper texture. A small RSS satellite dish icon in the corner, emitting a gentle wave. Calm teal accent color. Feels like a Sunday morning reading nook.

**CTA Button:** "Start Reading →"

**Skip link:** "Skip setup" (bottom right)

---

## Screen 2 — Add Your First Feed

**Headline:** What do you like to read?

**Body:** Paste a feed URL — any RSS, Atom, or JSON feed — and Folio will subscribe instantly. Search for popular feeds or add from your existing reader using an OPML file.

**Illustration:** A centered input field mockup (URL bar style) with a feed URL typed in. Below it, 3 floating "cards" showing popular feed examples: a blog icon, a news icon, a newsletter icon. Arrows point from the input to the cards to show subscription. Teal primary color.

**CTA Button:** "Add Feed URL…"

**Secondary link:** "Import OPML file"

---

## Screen 3 — Read Your Way

**Headline:** Designed for long reads.

**Body:** Folio's reader mode strips away everything except the text. Choose your font size, switch to dark mode, and save articles to read later — even offline.

**Illustration:** A split comparison — left: a cluttered web browser with ads and navigation bars; right: a clean Folio reader view showing the same article with just text and a subtle header. An arrow pointing from left to right with a "→" feel. The contrast tells the story without words.

**CTA Button:** "Next →"

---

## Screen 4 — All Set

**Headline:** Your feed is ready.

**Body:** Add more feeds anytime with `⌘N`. Use `⌘⇧R` to mark everything as read. Enjoy your reading time.

**Illustration:** A cozy illustration of an open Folio reader with a cup of coffee beside it (a subtle nod to the "Sunday morning" vibe). The app is showing an unread article. Soft warm tones. Small sparkles or a gentle checkmark badge.

**CTA Button:** "Open Folio"
